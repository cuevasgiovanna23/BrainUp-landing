Who are we 🤔

We are BrainUp, an app made to help you if you feel stuck with something in school, any subject that you need help with, such as Math, Language (literature), History and Chemistry. BrainUp was created by a group of high school girls as part of the Technovation Girls challenge. We invite you to complete this survey about BrainUp, we will also share your comment about BrainUp on social media, help us grow and help more students!

How to sign up 👀

To be able to use our application, scan the QR code you see above, this will take you to our app or simply click on "start". Next you’re going to put your email and a password for your account’s security. Now you can start using our app!

How to use our app 😎

First you are going to select your age range and the subject that your stuck with. Then, our app is going to show you a video based on your age and subject elections. You will have challenges that you will have to overcome, they are challenges appropriate to your age and subject, educational games, a tips assistant, random questions, and a super practical notebook.
