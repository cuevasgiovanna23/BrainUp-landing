Who are we 🤔

We are BrainUp, an app made to help you if you feel stuck with something in school, any subject that you need help with, such as Math, Language, History, Physics, Chemistry and Technology.

How to sign up 👀

You are going to download our app in your phone touching the icon on your screen’s top right corner. Next you’re going to put your email and a password for your account’s security. Now you can start using our app!

How to use our app 😎

First you are going to select your age range and the subject that your stuck with. Then, our app is going to show you a video based on your age and subject elections. You´ll have daily objetives that you have to achive to reach your studying goals.
